Individual Sector Performance in the Third Quarter Of 2018

HEALTH CARE: The Health Care sector rose with a return of 14.53%, extending its slight rise of 2.66% in the second quarter of 2018 for a combined return of 17.58% over both quarters. The top performers were Life Sciences Tools & Services and Pharmaceuticals, with returns of 19.69%, 16.98%, respectively. The worst performers were Biotechnology and Health Care Technology, returning 11.01%, 7.73%, respectively.
INDUSTRIALS: The Industrials sector rose with a return of 10.00%, reversing its slight fall of -3.66% in the second quarter of 2018 for a combined return of 5.97% over both quarters. The top performers were Airlines and Trading Companies & Distributors, with returns of 19.18%, 16.33%, respectively. The worst performers were Building Products and Industrial Conglomerates, returning 2.62%, 2.52%, respectively.
TELECOMMUNICATION SERVICES: The Telecommunication Services sector rose with a return of 9.94%, reversing its slight fall of -2.33% in the second quarter of 2018 for a combined return of 7.38% over both quarters. The sole component of the Telecommunication Services sector was Diversified Telecommunication Services with a return of 7.16%, respectively.
INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY: The Information Technology sector rose with a return of 8.80%, extending its rise of 6.75% in the second quarter of 2018 for a combined return of 16.14% over both quarters. The top performers were Technology Hardware, Storage & Peripherals and Software, with returns of 19.93%, 13.80%, respectively. The worst performers were Semiconductors & Semiconductor Equipment and Internet Software & Services, returning 3.68%, -4.63%, respectively.
CONSUMER DISCRETIONARY: The Consumer Discretionary sector rose with a return of 8.18%, extending its rise of 7.84% in the second quarter of 2018 for a combined return of 16.66% over both quarters. The top performers were Diversified Consumer Services and Specialty Retail, with returns of 14.15%, 11.71%, respectively. The worst performers were Household Durables and Automobiles, returning -9.33%, -12.76%, respectively.
CONSUMER STAPLES: The Consumer Staples sector rose with a return of 5.70%, reversing its slight fall of -2.34% in the second quarter of 2018 for a combined return of 3.23% over both quarters. The top performers were Food & Staples Retailing and Household Products, with returns of 12.08%, 7.52%, respectively. The worst performers were Food Products and Personal Products, returning 0.34%, 0.06%, respectively.
FINANCIALS: The Financials sector rose slightly with a return of 4.36%, reversing its slight fall of -3.58% in the second quarter of 2018 for a combined return of 0.62% over both quarters. The top performers were Diversified Financial Services and Insurance, with returns of 14.38%, 7.01%, respectively. The worst performers were Banks and Capital Markets, returning 3.79%, -2.10%, respectively.
UTILITIES: The Utilities sector rose slightly with a return of 2.39%, extending its slight rise of 2.80% in the second quarter of 2018 for a combined return of 5.25% over both quarters. Ranked by performance, the components of the Utilities sector were Independent Power and Renewable Electricity Producers, Electric Utilities and Multi-Utilities with returns of 13.86%, 2.22% and 1.69%, respectively.
REAL ESTATE: The Real Estate sector traded nearly flat with a return of 0.86%, extending its rise of 5.13% in the second quarter of 2018 for a combined return of 6.03% over both quarters. Ranked by performance, the components of the Real Estate sector were Equity Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITs) and Real Estate Management & Development with returns of 1.05% and -7.62%, respectively.
ENERGY: The Energy sector traded nearly flat with a return of 0.61%, extending its rise of 12.69% in the second quarter of 2018 for a combined return of 13.39% over both quarters. Ranked by performance, the components of the Energy sector were Oil Gas & Consumable Fuels and Energy Equipment & Services with returns of 1.54% and -5.90%, respectively.
MATERIALS: The Materials sector traded nearly flat with a return of 0.36%, extending its slight rise of 2.05% in the second quarter of 2018 for a combined return of 2.42% over both quarters. Ranked by performance, the components of the Materials sector were Chemicals, Containers & Packaging, Metals & Mining and Construction Materials with returns of 3.19%, 1.71%, -12.61% and -15.75%, respectively.
